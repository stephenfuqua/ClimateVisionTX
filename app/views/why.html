We didn't get here overnight. But we must stop here (virtually) overnight. That is a hard truth to accept.

But it is not too late to make a difference. It is not too late to express the best of what it means to be human: to care for each other. To recognize the intrinsic worth of another soul. To love life: all that is created. To leave the world a better place.

Why change our personal habits, communal priorities, and institutional regulations? Because we love our children, our neighbors, and our planet. Because action is where we will find our hope and joy. And because action can work: we saved the Bald Eagle, reduced acid rain, and stopped the growth of the ozone hole. We can do this.

"Let’s work together to make this planet earth environmentally sustainable so that our succeeding generations – children after children – will live peacefully. There is no Plan B because we do not have planet B."
U.N. Secretary General Ban Ki-moon
